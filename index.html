Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Do I need to be in shape to join?

Not at all! We welcome members of all fitness levels. Our trainers will help you start at your current level and progress at your own pace.

What equipment do I need to bring?

Just bring yourself, workout clothes, and a water bottle. We provide all equipment, towels, and lockers. Premium and Elite members get complimentary towels.

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes! All memberships can be cancelled with 30 days notice. No long-term contracts or hidden fees.

Do you offer nutrition guidance?

Yes! Premium members get monthly nutrition consultations, and Elite members receive unlimited access to our registered dietitian and custom meal plans.

What are your operating hours?

Basic and Premium members: Mon-Fri 5AM-11PM, Sat-Sun 6AM-10PM. Elite members have 24/7 access to the facility.

Do you offer trial sessions?

Yes! We offer a free 7-day trial for new members. Come in, try our facilities, and see if we're the right fit for you.
